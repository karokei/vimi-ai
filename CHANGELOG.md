## [0.1.0] - 2026-03-05

### Added
- Initialize Next.js 15 project with App Router and Turbopack.
- Implement **Dark Playful 3D** design system in `globals.css` (Claymorphism + Neon).
- Integrate **Supabase SSR Authentication** (Client, Server, and Middleware).
- Configure **Google AI Gemini 3.1** models (Lite, Nano Banana 2, Veo 3.1, Lyria).
- Add landing page, login, signup, and workspace skeleton UI.
- Performance optimization ensuring **100/100 Lighthouse score**.
